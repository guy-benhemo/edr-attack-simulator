# Guardz Messaging & Terminology

## Core Terminology

**Tagline:**
"Unified Detection & Response Built for MSPs & IT Service Providers"

**One-sentence description** (for interviews, presentations, podcasts):
"Guardz unifies detection and response in an AI-powered platform, streamlining cybersecurity for MSPs and IT service providers to protect clients and grow their business."

**Short description** (for general use):
"Guardz empowers MSPs and IT Service Providers to protect their clients effectively and efficiently. Offering a unified platform with automated detection and response ensures digital assets, emails, endpoints, data and identities are secure, allowing businesses to focus on growth."

**Elevator pitch:**
"Guardz provides MSPs and IT professionals with an AI-powered cybersecurity unified platform designed to secure and insure small businesses against cyberattacks. Our automatic detection and response platform protects users, emails, endpoints, cloud, and data. By simplifying cybersecurity management, we enable businesses to focus on growth while reducing security complexities."

## Key Phrases

Use these phrases consistently across all materials:

- "Built for MSPs, Securing SMBs"
- "AI-Powered Security and Insurance"
- "Quick Onboarding, Rapid Growth"
- "Unified Detection & Response"
- "Streamline cybersecurity"
- "Protect clients and grow their business"

## Product Messaging

When describing the platform:
"A unified, multi-tenant platform built specifically for MSPs. Our platform streamlines cybersecurity management with automated detection and response, allowing MSPs to offer their clients robust protection without the traditional burden of high costs and complex operations."

## Brand Values to Reinforce

Every piece of messaging should reflect one or more of:

1. **Security** — Unified protection of Users, Endpoints, Email, Cloud Data, Web, Internet Assets, and building Awareness culture
2. **Client-Centric Commitment** — Understanding and addressing MSP needs: security offering, daily operations, business conduct
3. **Trust** — Integrity, transparency, reliability
4. **Empowerment** — AI-powered platform to secure clients from the inside out

## Audience

- **Primary**: MSPs (Managed Service Providers) and IT Service Providers
- **End beneficiaries**: SMBs (Small & Medium Businesses)
- Always frame Guardz as built FOR MSPs, protecting SMBs

# Guardz Product & Service Content

## Service Description

Guardz is a holistic cybersecurity platform that provides enterprise-grade cyber technology
and makes it accessible to SMB/SMEs. We empower MSPs and IT professionals to secure small
businesses inside out from common threats such as phishing, ransomware/malware, data loss,
user risk, etc. in a single unified solution.

## Security Modules

### Compromised Data (Dark Web Monitoring)
Tracks leaked credentials found in data dumps and lists on the dark web. Guardz scrapes
and analyzes sources that monitor the dark web for malicious activity targeting businesses
and users. Analyzes domains related to the company and surfaces leaked credentials as
high-severity issues.

### External Surface
Performs vulnerability assessments analyzing networks, servers, and endpoints to identify
weaknesses — open ports, misconfigurations, and unpatched software. Provides comprehensive
vulnerability reports to help businesses prioritize and address security risks.

### Cloud Data Security
Monitors and protects cloud environments with real-time visibility into cloud application
security. Detects misconfigurations, monitors user access privileges, discovers externally
exposed data, and identifies potential threats to safeguard integrity and confidentiality
of cloud data.

### Cybersecurity Awareness & Phishing Simulations
Comprehensive awareness training programs educating employees about cyber threats including
phishing attacks, social engineering techniques, and password security. AI-driven Phishing
Simulations provide an adaptable and flexible approach to testing user awareness and
identifying education gaps.

### Web Browsing Protection
Blocks access to malicious sites, monitors downloads for potential threats, and provides
real-time threat intelligence. Secures the browsing experience against web-based attacks
that could compromise systems and data.

### Device Protection (Endpoints)
Advanced antivirus and anti-malware solutions with real-time scanning, behavior-based
analysis, and automatic threat response mechanisms to detect and mitigate malicious
activities on devices. Prevents malware infections and unauthorized access attempts.

### Email Security
Robust filters, anti-spam measures, and email quarantine leveraging machine learning
algorithms to identify and block suspicious emails. Reduces the risk of successful
email-based attacks including phishing, ransomware, and business email compromise.
